Central MP's Large Educational Institute

Oriental Institute of Science and Technology Bhopal was established in 1995 and is located in Bhopal, Madhya Pradesh. Oriental Institute Bhopal is one of the leading institutes which is approved by the AICTE and the government of Madhya Pradesh. OIST Bhopal is accredited by the National Board of Accreditation (NBA), New Delhi. OIST offers undergraduate and postgraduate programmes across various streams, including Engineering and Management. The flagship course offered at the Oriental Institute of Science and Technology is BTech. OIST Bhopal has collaborated with top industry partners. Oriental Institute of Science and Technology, Bhopal trained and experienced faculty members provide education to students. OIST has an exquisite infrastructure which is well-equipped with all the necessary amenities. The institute organises extracurricular activities such as sports fest, techno-cultural and others
